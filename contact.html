Frequently Asked Questions

How can I enroll in your courses?

+

You can enroll in our courses directly through our website. Browse the courses page, select your preferred course, and complete the payment process. You'll receive immediate access to the course materials.

Do you offer customized workshops for schools?

+

Yes, we offer customized workshops tailored to the specific needs of schools and educational institutions. Contact us with your requirements, and we'll design a workshop that fits your students' needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets through our secure Razorpay integration.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with a course?

+

We offer a 7-day refund policy for all our courses. If you're not satisfied with the course content, you can request a full refund within 7 days of purchase.
