Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I enroll in your courses?

+

You can enroll in our courses directly through our website. Browse the courses page, select your preferred course, and complete the secure payment process using Razorpay. After successful payment, you'll receive immediate access to the course materials, including videos, resources, and assignments. You can learn at your own pace and access the course from any device.

Do you offer customized workshops for schools?

+

Yes! We specialize in creating customized workshops tailored to the specific needs of schools and educational institutions. Our workshops cover career guidance, skill development, personality development, and subject-specific training. We work closely with school administrators to design programs that align with your curriculum and student requirements. Contact us with your specific needs, and we'll create a customized proposal.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various secure payment methods through our Razorpay integration including:
• Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay)
• Net Banking (All major Indian banks)
• UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
• Digital Wallets
• EMI options available for higher-value courses
All payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with a course?

+

We offer a 7-day no-questions-asked refund policy for all our courses. If you're not satisfied with the course content or quality, you can request a full refund within 7 days of purchase. After 7 days, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis for valid reasons. To request a refund, simply contact our support team with your purchase details.

How long do I have access to the courses?

+

You get lifetime access to all the courses you purchase! This means you can revisit the course materials anytime, even after completion. We regularly update our course content to keep it current with industry trends, and you'll receive all future updates at no additional cost. Learn at your own pace without any time pressure.

Do you provide certificates after course completion?

+

Yes, we provide digital certificates of completion for all our courses. To receive the certificate, you need to complete all course modules and assignments with a minimum passing grade. Our certificates are professionally designed and can be shared on LinkedIn, included in resumes, or printed for physical copies. They serve as valuable credentials for your career development.

What support do you offer during the course?

+

We provide comprehensive support throughout your learning journey:
• 24/7 access to course materials
• Q&A forum for course-related questions
• Weekly live doubt-clearing sessions
• Email support with 24-hour response time
• Career guidance sessions with experts
• Community access for peer learning
