Blog — Reports

Two event reports from Namakkal Kongu Matriculation Higher Secondary School.

Career Guidance
Event: Workshop for 12th standard students

We are thrilled to announce our workshop, in collaboration with Namakkal Kongu Matriculation Higher Secondary School, specifically designed for 12th standard students. This exciting event is scheduled to take place on October 14th, 2024.

Our team, Infront Of Us, is eager to share our knowledge and expertise to guide these young minds in shaping their careers. This interactive session aims to provide valuable insights and guidance, empowering them to make informed decisions about their future.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to the management and principal of Namakkal Kongu Matriculation Higher Secondary School for this wonderful opportunity. Your support and trust in our team are greatly appreciated.

We look forward to making a positive impact on the lives of these young individuals.

Science Expo
Event: Science Expo — Judge: Founder & CEO

Hello followers! This is from the Founder & CEO of Infront of Us.

I am thrilled to share that I have been invited as a judge for the science expo held on October 15, 2024, at Namakkal Kongu Matriculation Higher Secondary School. As a student, I spent 12 incredible years at this institution, participating in numerous expos and events at various colleges, schools, and institutions. These experiences not only broadened my knowledge but also helped me develop essential skills like communication, leadership, and time management.

Today, I am overwhelmed with gratitude as I return to my roots, not as a participant, but as a judge. This is truly a dream come true moment.

I extend my heartfelt gratitude to the management and Principal Mrs. Geetha Ma'am for bestowing this honor upon me. I believe this platform will enable me to give back to my community and inspire the next generation of innovators.
